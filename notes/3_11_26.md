- Land cover
    - Process of characterizing the physical material at Earth's surface into distinct classes
    - Via statistical/ML techniques to automatically identify classes across large areas
    - Whole classification/ML thing is learning:
        - Spectral signatures (NDIV, etc.)
        - Spatial patterns (parking lots, fields, bodies of water, etc.)

- Land cover vs. land use
    - Trees are **land cover** | Hunting / christmas trees / parks are **land use**
    - Land cover: Water | Land use: Fishing / pools / sewage
    - Land cover: Built-up | Land use: Residential / commercial / schools
    - Crops are land cover as they are different from looks and changes throughout the year but could make an argument for either land cover / land use
